SUMMARY
=======

This article contains a listing of the files included in the downloadable
Microsoft Exchange Inbox Update for Windows 95 (Exupdusa.exe) file.

For information about how to obtain the Microsoft Exchange Inbox Update for
Windows 95, please see the following article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

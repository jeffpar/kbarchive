Q325043: Fighter Ace II: "Fighter Ace 2.5 Is No Longer Available on MSN"

Article: Q325043
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbimu
Last Modified: 08-JUL-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Fighter Ace II 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Microsoft Fighter Ace II, and then click Play on the startup
screen, you are directed to Zone.com, where the following message appears:

  As of May 20th, 2002, Fighter Ace 2.5 is no longer available on the MSN
  Gaming Zone.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, do either of the following:

- Click Training on the startup screen. In Training mode, you can play single
  player through limited training exercises.

-or-

- Purchase and install Microsoft Combat Flight Simulator, which can be played
  single player or multiplayer online.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For information about Fighter Ace III (which is not supported by Microsoft),
visit the following VR1 Entertainment Web site:

  http://fighterace.vr1.com/

Q253163: Games: Screen Turns Black, Program Stops Responding, or Err Msg

Article: Q253163
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 2.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
- Microsoft Golf 1999 Edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings or Microsoft Golf
1999 Edition, you may experience any of the following symptoms:

- The screen may turn black after the opening movie is played.

- The program may stop responding (hang) when the startup screen is displayed.

- You may receive the following error message

  <Program> caused an invalid page fault in module win5fcl.dll.

  where <program> is the name of the executable file for the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if FamilyConnect Internet filtering software is
installed on the computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, remove the FamilyConnect filtering software:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Add/Remove Programs.

3. Click FamilyConnect Filtering, and then click Add/Remove.

  You are prompted to dial a toll-free telephone number to obtain a special
  password to remove the filtering program and cancel your account.

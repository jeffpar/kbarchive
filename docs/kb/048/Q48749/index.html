Q48749: Ocean 300 Feet Above Sea Level North of Port Angeles

Article: Q48749
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 30-JUL-2001

SUMMARY
=======

The altitude of the ocean north of Port Angeles is incorrectly shown as 300 feet
above sea level in Flight Simulator Version 4.00.

Microsoft is researching this problem and will post new information here as it
becomes available.

Q159021: NBA Full Court Press: Player Stats Not Displayed

Article: Q159021
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 08-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft NBA Full Court Press for Windows, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play NBA Full Court Press, the player statistics are not displayed at
the bottom of the screen during a game.

MORE INFORMATION
================

When the Windows display settings are set to 640 x 480 resolution, NBA Full
Court Press is not capable of displaying the information on the screen small
enough to include the player statistics during a game.

To correct this problem, change the Windows display settings to greater than 640
x 480 resolution, then select a higher display area in NBA Full Court Press.

To change the display settings in Windows, do the following:

1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

2. Double-click Display.

3. Click the Settings tab.

4. Drag the slider bar in the Desktop Area or Screen Area box to an area greater
  than 640 x 480.

5. Click Apply, then OK to the message that says Windows will resize your
  desktop.

6. When the desktop changes area size, a new message will appear to confirm the
  change. Click OK to confirm the new settings, then OK again to close the
  Display Properties.

To change the Display Area in NBA Full Court Press, do the following:

1. Start NBA Full Court Press.

2. On the Startup screen, click Options.

3. From the Options menu, click Display.

4. In Display, select a resolution button other than 640 x 480.

5. Click OK.

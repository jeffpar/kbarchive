Q243064: Age of Empires II: Duplicate Pages or Missing Pages in Manual

Article: Q243064
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:2.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbdocerr kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 12-JUN-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings, version 2.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

Some copies of Age of Empires II: The Age of Kings may include a manual that
contains duplicates of certain pages or is missing certain pages.

RESOLUTION
==========

If you have a defective manual, please call Microsoft Customer Service at (800)
426-9400 to request a replacement manual. Microsoft Customer Service hours are
Monday through Friday, 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M. Pacific time.

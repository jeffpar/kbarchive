Q233245: Streets and Trips 2000: Cannot Use GPS Device with Program

Article: Q233245
Product(s): Microsoft Automap
Version(s): WINDOWS:
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbimu
Last Modified: 27-JUL-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

You cannot use a Global Positioning System (GPS) device with Microsoft Expedia
Streets and Trips 2000.

MORE INFORMATION
================

Expedia Streets and Trips 2000 does not support GPS technology.

Q233417: Microsoft PowerPoint 2000 Step by Step Comments and Corrections

Article: Q233417
Product(s): Microsoft Press
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbdocfix kbdocerr
Last Modified: 09-DEC-2000

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- MSPRESS Microsoft PowerPoint 2000 Step by Step ISBN 1-57231-972-0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article contains comments, corrections, and information about known errors
relating to the Microsoft Press book Microsoft PowerPoint 2000 Step by Step,
ISBN 1-57231-972-0.

The following topics are covered:

- CD-ROM: Readme.txt Instructions For Decker Presentations May Not Work

- Pages 64-65: Add First Step and Renumber Steps Accordingly

MORE INFORMATION
================

CD-ROM: Readme.txt Instructions For Decker Presentations May Not Work
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The steps presented in the Readme.txt for viewing the Decker Presentation Center
Video workshops from the companion CD-ROM may not work for all systems (notably
Windows 95, and possibly some NT 4.0 systems and Windows 2000 system).

On some systems, typing D:\Presentation Center in the Start-Run menu will return
the following error:

  cannot find the file 'd:\presentation' or one of its components

The instructions in the Readme.txt should be modified as follows:
3. Type "D:\Presentation Center" (where D is your CD-ROM drive letter) and press
the Enter key. The statement must be enclosed in quotation marks.
4. This will open a Windows Explorer window.

Note: The instructions presented in the book, pages xviii-xix, which are slightly
different, will work on all systems.


Pages 64-65: Add First Step and Renumber Steps Accordingly
----------------------------------------------------------

Add a new first step to the procedure "Collapse and Expand the Outline."

Add:
1. If necessary, on the View menu, point to Toolbars, and then click Outlining to
display the Outlining Toolbar.

Then renumber the rest of the current steps 1-7 to be steps 2-8.


Microsoft Press is committed to providing informative and accurate books. All
comments and corrections listed above are ready for inclusion in future
printings of this book. If you have a later printing of this book, it may
already contain most or all of the above corrections.

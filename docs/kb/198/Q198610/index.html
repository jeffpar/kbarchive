Q198610: AoE: Err Msg "Could Not Initialize Graphics" on ViewSonic VPA150

Article: Q198610
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kberrmsg aoe kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 20-JUN-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Age of Empires, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you attempt to start Microsoft Age of Empires, you may receive the
following error message:

  Could not initialize graphics system. Make sure that your video card and
  driver are compatible with DirectDraw.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

- You are using a ViewSonic VPA150 monitor.
- DirectDraw is disabled in the monitor's pivoting software.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, enable DirectDraw in the monitor's pivoting software. To
do this, follow these steps:

1. Click the WinPortrait icon on the taskbar, and then click DirectDraw.

2. When you are prompted if you want to enable DirectDraw, click Yes.

3. When you are prompted to restart the computer, do so.

================

For additional information about this error message, click the article numbers
below to view the articles in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

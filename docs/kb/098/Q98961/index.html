Q98961: Mac Fax: Compatible Modems

Article: Q98961
Product(s): Microsoft Mail For Appletalk Networks
Version(s): WINDOWS:3.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 09-NOV-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Mail for AppleTalk Networks, version 3.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following modems are compatible with version 3.0 of the Microsoft Mail for
AppleTalk Network Gateway to Fax.

  AppleFax Modem from Apple Computer

  OrchidFax Modem from Orchid Technologies

  FlexFax Modem from Circuit Research

The FlexFax modem is the only modem available for retail sale. The AppleFax and
the OrchidFax modems may be available from a reseller organization such as Sun
Electronics or Pre-Owned Electronics.

Circuit Research, which is based in Nashua, New Hampshire, can be contacted by
calling (603) 880-4000.

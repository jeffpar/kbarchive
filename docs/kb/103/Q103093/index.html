Q103093: List of Color Pictures in Cinemania

Article: Q103093
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1992 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 08-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1992 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Cinemania contains 31 color images. These color images require a non-pallatized
color video driver installed in Windows. A 256-color video driver is
recommended.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To display a color image in Cinemania, select one of the titles listed below
from either Movie Listings or Gallery.

  Amadeus
  Annie Hall
  Becket
  Ben Hur (1959)
  Cinema Paradiso
  Edward Scissorhands
  Empire Strikes Back
  Godfather III
  Handmaids Tale, The
  House Party
  Kiss of the Spider Woman
  Krays
  Marriage of Maria Brown
  Matewan
  Miller's Crossing
  Mountains of the Moon
  Mr. and Mrs. Bridge
  My Left Foot
  Narrow Margin
  Network
  Pacific Heights
  Pump Up the Volume
  Russia House
  Sheik
  Stanley and Iris
  Ten Commandments
  Terms of Endearment
  Total Recall
  Two Jakes, The
  Young Guns II
  Zorba the Greek

Additional query words: 1992 multi media multimedia multi-media nonpallatized

Q61772: Hewlett-Packard Persuasive Presentations Font Cartridge

Article: Q61772
Product(s): Microsoft Windows 95.x Retail Product
Version(s): WINDOWS:2.x,3.0,3.0a,3.1,3.11
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 18-DEC-2000

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Windows versions 2.03, 2.1, 2.11, 3.0, 3.0a, 3.1, 3.11 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Below is a description of the capabilities of the Hewlett-Packard (HP)
Persuasive Presentations Font Cartridge.

The font cartridge supports a print resolution of 300 x 300 dots per inch (dpi)
and has an average lifetime of 500 insertions. Separate fonts are required for
landscape and portrait. Some fonts may not be available in both orientations on
a specific cartridge.

For more information about this font cartridge, please contact Hewlett-Packard.

MORE INFORMATION
================

The following explains Persuasive Presentations Cartridge capabilities:

--------      ----    -------  -----         -----  ----------

Presentation  Bold    P and L  10            14     ASCII, Legal

Presentation  Bold    P and L  8.1           16     ASCII, Legal

Presentation  Bold    P and L  6.5           18     ASCII, Legal

Presentation  Bold    P and L  5.7           24     ASCII, Legal

Helv Outline  Black   P and L  Proportional  24     ASCII, Legal

Serifa        Black   P and L  Proportional  24     ASCII, Legal

Line Draw     Medium  P and L  10            14     Line Draw

Q214549: CC III: Screen Turns Black and Computer Hangs When Playing Game

Article: Q214549
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbenv cc msgamekbfaq
Last Modified: 12-APR-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Close Combat III: The Russian Front 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Close Combat III: The Russian Front, the screen may suddenly turn
black and the computer may stop responding (hang).

CAUSE
=====

This issue can occur if you have a Creative Labs Graphics Blaster Exxtreme video
adapter installed in your computer and the game resolution is set to 1600 x
1200.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, install the latest video driver available for your video
adapter. You can obtain the latest video driver for the Graphics Blaster
Exxtreme video adapter from the following Creative Labs Web site:

http://www.soundblaster.com

You can also work around this issue by lowering the game's screen resolution. To
do this, follow these steps:

1. Start Close Combat III: The Russian Front.

2. On the startup screen, click Options.

3. Click the General tab.

4. Click a lower resolution in the Screen Resolution box under Performance
  Options, and then click OK.

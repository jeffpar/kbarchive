Q252802: Allegiance: Player Moves Erratically with Quick Bursts of Speed

Article: Q252802
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbdisplay igz kbimu msgamekbfaq
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Allegiance, another player may move erratically with
quick bursts of speed.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if the affected player is experiencing a high degree of
latency in their Internet connection.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, the affected player must reduce his or her Internet
latency.

For information about how to reduce Internet latency, click the article number
below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge Base:

  Q232392 Zone.com: High Latency in Game Lobbies or When Playing Games

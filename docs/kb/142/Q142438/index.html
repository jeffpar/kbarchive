Q142438: PPT7: Frequently Asked Questions about Genigraphics

Article: Q142438
Product(s): Microsoft PowerPoint for Windows
Version(s): WINDOWS:7.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdta
Last Modified: 18-FEB-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft PowerPoint for Windows 95, version 7.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


SUMMARY
=======

This article contains some of the most frequently asked questions about
Genigraphics.


MORE INFORMATION
================

Q. How do I install the Genigraphics Wizard?

A. The "Genigraphics Wizard and GraphicsLink" software comes with
  PowerPoint. You may or may not have it installed, depending upon the
  options you chose when installing PowerPoint. If you need to install
  the "Genigraphics Wizard and the GraphicsLink" software, follow
  these steps:

  1. On the Windows 95 Start menu, point to Settings, and then click
     Control Panel. Double-click the Add/Remove Programs icon.

  2. Click the Install/Uninstall tab, click Microsoft PowerPoint or
     Microsoft Office, and click Add/Remove.

  3. Click Add/Remove.

  4. When using PowerPoint 97, click Genigraphics and GraphicsLink from
     the Maintenance Mode options dialog box. When using Office 97,
     click Microsoft PowerPoint and then click Change Options. Click
     Genigraphics and GraphicsLink.

  5. Click OK to return to the Maintenance Mode dialog box. Click
     Continue and Setup installs Genigraphics Wizard and GraphicsLink.

Q. How do I set up my slides to print to Genigraphics?

A. It's important to select the proper setting for your slide before
  you begin. On the File menu, click Slide Setup. Choose "Slide sized
  for 35mm Slides."

Q. Which fonts does Genigraphics support?

A. Genigraphics supports the standard 35 PostScript fonts and the
  standard TrueType fonts provided with Windows and PowerPoint. For
  unsupported PostScript fonts, you must provide Genigraphics with
  copies of your fonts.

  The supported fonts are:

  Standard PostScript Fonts
  -------------------------
  Avant Garde
  Bookman
  Courier
  Helvetica
  Helvetica Narrow
  Palatino
  Symbol
  Times
  NewCentury Schoolbook
  Zapf Chancery
  Zapf Dingbats

  TrueType Fonts
  -------------------------

  Arial
  Arial Narrow
  Century Schoolbook
  Symbol
  Book Antiqua
  Time New Roman
  Monotype Corsiva

  Any unrestricted TrueType

Q. How do I prepare my files for Genigraphics?

A. Follow the steps below:

  1. From the File menu, point to Send To, and then click Genigraphics.

  2. The Genigraphics Wizard walks you through each step. Be careful
     to answer all questions (for example, what output you need, your
     order, how you would like your order processed, and shipping and
     billing information).

Q. Genigraphics has a variety of outputs to choose from. What are the
  differences?

A. They include:

     35mm Slides:      Genigraphics provides high quality
                       slide imaging. They also offer Double
                       sets and Slide Duplicates.

     Color Overheads:  Genigraphics has two to choose from.
                       Colorvues are the company standard, using
                       Digital Photographic continuous tone
                       output. Qwikvues use a thermal wax process
                       at 300 dpi output. Qwikvues are better for
                       one-time presentations.

     Color Prints:     These are produced from the same two processes
                       as Color Overheads. Slick Prints use 11-by-14-
                       inch display prints or 12-by-18-inch office
                       posters and Color Handouts, which are similar
                       to the quality of the Quikvues (color
                       overheads.)

Q. What are the available shipping options?

A. They include:

     Saturday: In by 10 AM; returned by noon Monday.
     Rush: In by 7 PM; returned by noon next business day.
     Next Day: In by 1 PM; returned by noon next business day.
     Second Day: In by 5 PM; returned by noon the second business day.
     Plan Ahead: In by 5 PM; returned by noon in 3-7 business days.

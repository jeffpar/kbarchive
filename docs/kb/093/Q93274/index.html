Q93274: Setup: Control Panel Drivers Supplied

Article: Q93274
Product(s): Miscellaneous Windows Products
Version(s): 
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 11-SEP-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Windows Sound System, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

Video for Windows 1.0 comes with a variety of drivers that can be installed and
configured using the Drivers dialog box of the Windows Control Panel accessory.
These drivers are located on the Drivers disk and on the CD-ROM that ship with
the retail product.

The exact name of each of these drivers, as listed in the Drivers dialog box is
important in identifying whether driver was provided with Video for Windows.

Control Panel installable drivers on the CD-ROM, as listed in the Drivers dialog
box:

  Creative Sound Blaster Pro 2 Auxiliary Audio
  Creative Sound Blaster Pro 2 MIDI Synthesizer
  Creative Sound Blaster Pro 2 Wave and MIDI
  Creative Sound Blaster Pro Auxiliary Audio
  Creative Sound Blaster Pro MIDI Synthesizer
  Creative Sound Blaster Pro Wave and MIDI
  Pro AudSpec (orig) Mixer
  Pro AudSpec (orig) Win3.1 Wave/MIDI/Aux
  Pro AudSpec 16 SndBlaster Wave
  Pro AudSpec 16 Win 3.1 Wave/MIDI/Aux
  Pro AudSpec Plus Stereo FM synth
  Pro AudSpec Plus Win 3.1 Wave/MIDI/Aux
  [MCI] Pro AudSpec (ALL) Mixer Control

Control Panel installable drivers on the Drivers disk as listed in Drivers dialog
box:

  Creative Labs VideoBlaster Video Capture
  Intel Indeo(TM) Video Driver
  Microsoft Video 1 Compressor
  New Media Graphics SVW Video Capture
  Truevision Bravado Video Capture
  Truevision Targa16 Plus Video Capture
  [MCI] Microsoft Video for Windows
  [MCI] Pioneer 4200 Videodisc

Any other wording or spelling would be an indication that the driver was not
provided with Video for Windows.

Correct wording does not ensure that the driver is one of the drivers provided
with Video for Windows.

Q256226: Allegiance: Minimum System Requirements

Article: Q256226
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbimu
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Allegiance, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

This article lists the minimum system requirements that are needed to run
Microsoft Allegiance.

MORE INFORMATION
================

To run Allegiance, you need:

- A Multimedia PC with a Pentium 200 MHz or faster processor with a 3D
  accelerator card or a Pentium II 266 MHz without a 3D accelerator card
  (Pentium II 300 MHz or faster processor with a 3D accelerator card
  recommended)

- Microsoft Windows 95/98 with DirectX 7.0 API, Microsoft Windows NT 4.0 with
  Service Pack 6.0 or higher, or Microsoft Windows 2000.

- 32 megabytes (MB) of RAM (64 MB of RAM recommended)

- 270 MB of available hard drive space for minimum installation

- A quad-speed or faster CD-ROM drive

- A Super VGA video adapter and a Super VGA monitor capable of supporting
  16-bit color at a screen resolution of 800 x 600 pixels

- A DirectX-compatible joystick recommended

- A DirectX-compatible sound card

- Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device

- Microsoft Internet Explorer Web browser version 4.0 or later, or Netscape
  version 4.0 or later

- 28,800 bits-per-second (bps) or faster modem or a local area network (LAN)
  with TCP/IP or IPX protocol (56,000 bps recommended)

- Internet access*

*Internet connectivity and an active Zone.com membership are required for access
to the Allegiance Zone (optional). Free Zone membership is subject to the terms
of the Zone license agreement. The offer for one month of free trial of
Allegiance Zone on Zone.com is available with proof of purchase through 12/1/00.
A credit card is required. After your first free month, you can cancel your
Allegiance Zone membership. If you do not cancel your membership, it will
automatically continue for just $9.95 per month or the then-current subscription
rate (subject to change without notice). This offer is not combinable with other
offers. This offer is valid in the 50 United States, District of Columbia, and
Canada only. Internet-connect time charges may apply.

Q258390: Baseball 2001: Textures for the Players and Crowd Are Distorted

Article: Q258390
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 22-JUN-2000

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Baseball 2001 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft Baseball 2001, the textures for the players, the crowd,
or the field may be distorted.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if both of the following conditions are true:

- A Matrox Millennium G-400 video adapter is installed in the computer.

- The video driver for the Matrox Millennium G-400 video adapter is outdated.

RESOLUTION
==========

To resolve this issue, download and install the latest video driver for the
Matrox Millennium G-400 video adapter from the following Matrox Web site:

  http://www.matrox.com

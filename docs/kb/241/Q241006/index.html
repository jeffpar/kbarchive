Q241006: NBA 2000: Slow Frame Rate and Poor Graphics Performance

Article: Q241006
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): WINDOWS:1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 01-OCT-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft NBA Inside Drive 2000, you may experience a slow video
frame rate and poor graphics performance in the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if a video adapter based on the Riva TNT or 3Dfx Voodoo2
chipsets is installed in your computer.

RESOLUTION
==========

You may be able to resolve this issue if you disable the anti-aliasing and
sync-to-refresh rate features of your video adapter.

For information about how to disable these features, please refer to the printed
documentation provided with your video adapter or contact your hardware
manufacturer.

MORE INFORMATION
================

For information about how to contact your hardware manufacturer, click the
appropriate article number below to view the article in the Microsoft Knowledge
Base:

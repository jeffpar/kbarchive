Q250108: Asheron’s Call: Your Patron Has Been Deleted from the Game

Article: Q250108
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kberrmsg kbui igz kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 06-AUG-2002

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Asheron's Call, version 1.0 
- Microsoft Asheron's Call: Dark Majesty, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you start Asheron's Call and log in to Zone.com, you may receive the
following message:

  Your Patron <character name> has been deleted from the game.

In addition, your character is no longer bound to the Allegiance.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior occurs if your character's Patron deletes his/her character.

MORE INFORMATION
================

When a character's Patron deletes his/her character, all Allegiance information
is also deleted for that character's Vassals.

If those Vassals have Vassals of their own, they automatically become Monarchs of
their own Allegiance ranks.

If a Monarch deletes his/her character in Asheron's Call, the entire Monarchy is
dissolved.

For additional information about how character information is stored in Asheron's
Call, click the article number below to view the article in the Microsoft
Knowledge Base:

  Q249913 Asheron's Call: Description of How Character Information Is Stored

Q151057: Cinemania 96 Update Contents: May 96

Article: Q151057
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1996 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): 
Last Modified: 16-DEC-1999

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Cinemania for Windows 1996 edition 
- Microsoft Cinemania for the Macintosh 1996 edition 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SUMMARY
=======

The following article lists the movie titles included in the May 96 update of
Cinemania 1996:

Movie Titles
------------

Barb Wire - Celluloid Closet - Celtic Pride - Denise Calls Up - Getting Away with
Murder - Ghost in the Shell - Gospa - Hate - James and the Giant Peach - Jane
Eyre - Kids in the Hall: Brain Candy - Land and Freedom - Last Dance - Last
Supper - Mrs. Winterbourne - Mulholland Falls - Mystery Science Theater 3000:
The Movie - Pallbearer - Primal Fear - Substitute - Sunset Park - Truth About
Cats and Dogs - Young Poisoner's Handbook

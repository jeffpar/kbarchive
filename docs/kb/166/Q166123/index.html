Q166123: Mac Encarta 97: Collage Introductions and Sounds Stutter

Article: Q166123
Product(s): Microsoft Home Multimedia Titles
Version(s): 1997 edition
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kbhw kbmm kbsound kbusage kbprb kbHardware
Last Modified: 25-JUL-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft Encarta 97 Encyclopedia for Macintosh 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you open an Encarta collage, you may notice that the sounds skip or
stutter.

This occurs on a Power Macintosh (Power PC) computer running the Mac OS.

NOTE: Sound in the collages is disabled on computers equipped with Motorola 680x0
processors.


STATUS
======

Microsoft has confirmed this to be a problem in the 1997 edition of Microsoft
Encarta Encyclopedia for the Macintosh. Microsoft is researching this problem
and will post new information here as it becomes available.

Additional query words: macmm multi multimedia media

Q259955: StarLancer: Video Adapter Is Not Detected as Direct3D Compatible

Article: Q259955
Product(s): Microsoft Home Games
Version(s): 1.0
Operating System(s): 
Keyword(s): kb3rdparty kbdisplay kbimu msgame
Last Modified: 12-NOV-2001

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The information in this article applies to:

- Microsoft StarLancer, version 1.0 
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

SYMPTOMS
========

When you play Microsoft StarLancer, your video adapter may not be detected as a
Direct3D-compatible device. As a result, 3D Render Mode is configured to use the
Software Renderer when you play the game.

CAUSE
=====

This behavior can occur if any of the following video adapters are installed in
your computer:

- Matrox MGA Mystique

- Matrox Millennium II AGP PowerDesk

- S3 ViRGE PCI 325

These video adapters do not support all of the Direct3D features that are
required by StarLancer.

MORE INFORMATION
================

This behavior has also been reported with video adapters based on the S3 ViRGE
Vx/Dx/Gx chip sets.
